1. Introduction

Welcome to Vua Thao Duoc Automation ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our content management and publishing platform, including our integration with TikTok's API.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive your TikTok User ID, display name, and profile information via the TikTok OAuth process.
  • Content Data: Videos and metadata you submit through our platform for publishing to TikTok.
  • Access Tokens: TikTok access tokens required to perform actions on your behalf (e.g., posting videos). These are stored securely and used only for authorized operations.
  • Usage Data: Log data such as timestamps, IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited, collected automatically to maintain and improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your session securely.
  • Publish video content to your TikTok profile on your behalf, only with your explicit authorization.
  • Schedule and manage content publishing as configured by you.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve platform performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and TikTok's Platform Terms of Service.

4. TikTok API Data Usage

Our platform integrates with the TikTok API. Data obtained through TikTok's API is used solely to provide the features you request — specifically, publishing video content to your TikTok account. We do not use TikTok API data for advertising, resale, or any purpose beyond the direct functionality of this service.

We comply with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and all applicable platform policies.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services (e.g., hosting providers) that help us operate our platform, bound by confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. TikTok access tokens are stored only while your account is connected and are deleted upon disconnection or account deletion.
